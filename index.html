Welcome Message from the Principal

Welcome to Malanday National High School, “THE HOME OF TECHNOPRENEURS” and the only public secondary TECH-VOC school in the Division of Marikina City. As a secondary TECH-VOC school , we take pride of our TVE teachers who are NC Passers and TM Holders in different field of specialization. We envision our school to be a nurturing TECH-VOC school which prepares students for productive life and for college education. Thus, we are geared at preparing our learners to face the rigors of college education and more importantly, ready to face the actual challenges of the workplace.
